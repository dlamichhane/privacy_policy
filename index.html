FastGoal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to FastGoal, a mobile application designed to help you track your intermittent fasting schedules. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is handled when you use our application.

Information Collection

FastGoal collects certain types of data to improve your experience and to enable personalized advertisements. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent in the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Advertising Identifiers: Such as Google Advertising ID or Apple's IDFA

Your fasting schedules, timers, and statistics are stored locally on your device. You maintain control over this data and delete it at any time from the app.

Advertising and Data Sharing

FastGoal includes third-party advertising to support its development and maintenance. To provide relevant advertisements, certain data may be collected and shared with our advertising partners, including:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements within the app
  • Advertising identifiers for personalized ad delivery
  • Non-personal usage statistics to improve ad relevance

You can adjust your personalized ad preferences in Settings → Privacy Settings → Update Ad Preferences. Choosing to opt out of personalized ads will still show advertisements, but they will not be tailored to your interests.

Third-Party Services

FastGoal integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing these policies for additional information:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect the data collected through our app. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. Your personal fasting data is stored locally on your device, and we recommend keeping your device's operating system and the FastGoal app updated to the latest version.

Important Health Disclaimer

This app is designed solely to help users track fasting durations with a timer. It does not provide medical advice, fasting recommendations, or dietary guidance. The information presented is for tracking purposes only and should not be considered a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Before starting any fasting regimen, consult a qualified healthcare provider, especially if you have pre-existing medical conditions. By using this app, you acknowledge that fasting carries potential health risks, and you assume full responsibility for your health decisions. The developers of this app are not liable for any health issues, injuries, or damages resulting from its use.

The app does not guarantee specific results, and individual experiences may vary. Any third-party links or recommendations within the app are provided for convenience, and we do not endorse or control their content.

Children's Privacy

FastGoal is not specifically directed at children under the age of 17 We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we process. You can exercise many of these rights directly through the app's settings. If you have additional questions or requests, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the app. Significant changes will be notified through app updates.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Application includes:

2.1. Personal Data

Personally identifiable information, such as your email address, name, or other similar data, may be collected if you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., when contacting support or purchasing an app, if applicable). We generally do not require extensive personal data for the core functionality of the app.

2.2. Purchase Information

If you choose to make In-App Purchases, such as "Remove Ads," we use a third-party service, RevenueCat, to manage these purchases. RevenueCat may collect information necessary to process your transactions, which is governed by RevenueCat's privacy policy. We receive information from RevenueCat regarding your purchase status and entitlements (e.g., whether you have purchased an ad-free version).

We also store a record of your purchase status (e.g., if ads have been removed) and the relevant product identifier locally on your device using AsyncStorage to ensure the app reflects your purchases correctly. This locally stored information includes whether a purchase was made and the product ID.

Payment processing for In-App Purchases is handled by the respective app stores (Apple App Store or Google Play Store) and RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your credit card details.

2.3. Usage Data and Analytics

We may collect information about how you use the Application, such as the features you use, the screens you visit, and the actions you take. This may include device information (e.g., device model, operating system version) and log data (e.g., error logs, diagnostic information). This data is primarily used for improving the Application, troubleshooting, and understanding user engagement.

2.4. Device Information

We may collect information about your mobile device, such as your mobile device ID (if applicable and permitted), model, manufacturer, and operating system version. This information is used for optimizing the Application for your device and for analytics.
